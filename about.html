The Ongoing Pollution Crisis

by Meredith Olzem and Adam Laubach

The Earth's environmental health has gone significantly downhill. As a result animal species have gone extinct, communities have less food, and more. Our genuine concern prompted us to spread awareness about the general topic of pollution. Pollution has the ability to affect ecosystems and any organism that lives in it. This can cause species to decline, altering biodiversity. It also affects those who depend on the ocean for food, tourism, and income. The amount of plastic littered across the world right now is tremendous, so what will it be like for future generations? No change will cause not only animal decline, but human decline. To help bring awareness and change please share this website and consider donating to reliable organizations.

As society increases its consumerism and creates a growing number of nonessential products, the land, ocean, air, space, and ecosystems are increasingly damaged. By raising awareness and taking individual responsibility, we can make a meaningful change to protect and preserve our planet.
